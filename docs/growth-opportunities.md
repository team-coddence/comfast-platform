# Growth Opportunities — Path to 50,000 Customers

## 0. Definition of "customer" for this plan

- **Signed-up active user** (free or paid) within 30 days of launch.
- Paid sub-target: **1,000–2,500 paying customers** at $9–$29/mo by end of month 1.
- Activation gate: at least 1 social account connected AND 1 post published or scheduled.

## 2. Activation loops

1. **Signup → connect first account in <60s** — OAuth must be 2 clicks. Pre-select LinkedIn + X for the persona.
2. **First post in <3 minutes** — AI Composer offers 3 ready-to-publish hooks based on the user's bio.
3. **"Repurpose this" sharable output** — every generated post has a "shared via UniNet" subtle attribution toggle (default ON in free tier).
4. **Public roast/share** — "Let UniNet roast your last 5 posts" → instant shareable report.
5. **Streaks** — daily-posting streak badge with shareable card (Duolingo loop).

## 3. Viral loops (built into product)

- **Free tier watermark**: tiny "Made with UniNet" line at end of generated AI content (off in paid).
- **Public share page**: every post can be shared with a `uninet.app/p/<slug>` page (SEO + branding).
- **Referral program**: invite 3 friends → 1 month Pro free. 1-click X/LinkedIn invite from inside app.
- **Public templates marketplace**: users can publish their best-performing post templates → traffic loop.
- **Embed badge**: "Powered by UniNet" on creator websites in exchange for analytics credits.

## 4. Retention loops

- **Weekly Insights digest email** every Monday — "Your best post last week was X, schedule next 5".
- **AI Pilot mode** — once a user has 30+ posts, auto-suggest a full week of content for one-click approval.
- **Best-time-to-post engine** — recalculates weekly, becomes more valuable over time.
- **Content calendar lock-in** — visual calendar with 30+ scheduled posts is hard to leave.

## 6. Distribution moats to build during month 1

- **Integrations marketplace** scaffold: Notion, Zapier, RSS, Webflow, Ghost, Substack, Beehiiv → each integration is a partnership-marketing opportunity.
- **Public Changelog** + **Public Roadmap** — drives recurring visits & shares.
- **Open-source the prompt library** under MIT → GitHub stars → developer pipeline.
- **University ambassador program** (post-month-1): free Team plan for student orgs.

## 7. Risks to growth & mitigations

- **Spam abuse of free tier** → phone OTP, IP rate limits, content moderation pass on AI output.
- **Platform suspension** (X/Meta) for fake-looking automation → strict ToS adherence, no engagement automation.
- **AI cost spike** with virality → set per-user daily token caps, Haiku-first routing.
- **Onboarding drop-off** → analytics on every step; iterate hourly during launch week.
